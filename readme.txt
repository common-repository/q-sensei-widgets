= About the Plugin =

This Plugin allows you to quickly and easily include the useful features of the Q-Sensei Search Engine directly into your blog.

= Q-Sensei - Your Guide through the World's Information =
 
Q-Sensei introduces a new way of making information accessible. Its goal is to provide advanced 
search tools for the vast amount of information available nowadays on the Internet, in books, 
research articles, blogs, news, or wikis. They currently provide access to the first several 
million scholarly articles. Q-Sensei's approach applies innovative search technologies to 
various heterogeneous data pools; their users can easily browse through a data source as well 
as discover cross-references between individual records. 

Try out [Q-Sensei](http://www.qsensei.com "Your Guide through the World's Information")!


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Setup the Widget in the Widget-Section of your WP-admin panel
